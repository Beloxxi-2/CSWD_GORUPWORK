Meet the Artists

Wireless Festival 2025 brings together the biggest names in music for an unforgettable weekend of live performances. From global superstars to rising talents, our lineup spans genres like Afrobeat, Hip-Hop, R&B, and Drill, ensuring there's something for everyone.

Get ready to experience electrifying performances, soulful melodies, and high-energy beats as these incredible artists take the stage. Whether you're here to vibe with your favorite headliners or discover new sounds, Wireless Festival promises a musical journey like no other.

Scroll down to explore the full lineup and learn more about the artists performing this year. Don't forget to check out their Spotify previews and follow them on social media to stay connected!

Friday Lineup

Burna Boy

A global icon of Afrofusion, Burna Boy brings his infectious energy and genre-blending sound to the Wireless stage. From “Ye” to “Last Last,” expect powerful vocals, rhythmic grooves, and pure vibes as he delivers a world-class performance filled with passion, pride, and unstoppable Afrobeat heat.

Future

A true trap pioneer, Future’s dark melodies and heavy-hitting anthems have defined a generation. With chart-toppers like “Mask Off” and “Life Is Good,” expect a relentless set full of swagger, bass, and raw emotion.

Lil Baby

One of the biggest names in rap today, Lil Baby hits the Wireless stage with back-to-back anthems and unfiltered street energy. From “Drip Too Hard” to “The Bigger Picture,” his raw flow and crowd-commanding presence guarantee a performance you won’t want to miss.

Saturday Lineup

Central Cee

West London's own Central Cee returns with razor-sharp bars and drill-infused bangers that have taken the world by storm. Known for “Doja” and “Sprinter,” his flow, fashion, and stage presence are unmatched.

Lil Uzi Vert

A true rockstar of the new school, Lil Uzi Vert brings futuristic vibes, electric energy, and hits like “XO Tour Llif3” and “Just Wanna Rock.”

Sunday Lineup

Jorja Smith

Get ready for pure elegance and soulful energy as Jorja Smith brings her signature blend of R&B, jazz, and pop to the stage. With stunning vocals and hits like “Be Honest” and “Blue Lights,” her set promises to be an unforgettable moment of beauty and emotion.
